Workshop Programme

Training & Knowledge Transfer Sessions

Programme Overview

Our comprehensive workshop programme is designed to empower your team with the knowledge and skills needed to maximize the value of the ENMS platform. Through hands-on training, best practice sharing, and real-world case studies, participants gain practical expertise in energy management and Industry 4.0 technologies.

Available Workshops

ENMS Fundamentals (Half Day)

4 hours Up to 15 participants Beginner

Introduction to the ENMS platform, navigation, dashboard customization, and basic energy monitoring concepts. Perfect for new users and management staff.

  • Platform overview and user interface
  • Understanding energy dashboards
  • Creating custom views
  • Basic reporting and data export

Advanced Analytics & Optimization (Full Day)

8 hours Up to 12 participants Intermediate

Deep dive into data analysis, EnPI calculation, performance benchmarking, and energy optimization strategies.

  • Energy Performance Indicators (EnPIs)
  • Baseline establishment and normalization
  • Statistical analysis and forecasting
  • Identifying optimization opportunities
  • Machine learning predictions

ISO 50001 Implementation (2 Days)

16 hours Up to 10 participants Advanced

Comprehensive training on using the platform to meet ISO 50001 requirements, documentation, and certification preparation.

  • ISO 50001 standard overview
  • Energy review and planning
  • Documentation requirements
  • Internal audit preparation
  • Management review processes

Technical Integration Workshop (2 Days)

16 hours Up to 8 participants Technical

For IT staff and system integrators: IoT device integration, API usage, Node-RED workflow development, and system administration.

  • MQTT and IoT protocols
  • Device onboarding and configuration
  • Node-RED flow development
  • API integration and automation
  • Database queries and custom dashboards

Digital Product Passport Masterclass (1 Day)

8 hours Up to 12 participants Intermediate

Learn to create, manage, and leverage digital product passports for sustainability reporting and compliance.

  • DPP concepts and regulations
  • Product lifecycle tracking
  • Carbon footprint calculation
  • ESG reporting
  • Supply chain transparency

Workshop Format

All workshops combine theoretical knowledge with hands-on practice:

  • Interactive Presentations: Engaging content with real-world examples
  • Live Demonstrations: Platform features demonstrated in real-time
  • Practical Exercises: Participants work with actual platform instances
  • Q&A Sessions: Dedicated time for addressing specific questions
  • Case Studies: Learn from successful implementations
  • Resource Materials: Comprehensive documentation and guides provided

Delivery Options

  • On-Site: Training conducted at your facility
  • Virtual: Live online sessions with full interactivity
  • Hybrid: Combination of on-site and remote participants
  • Self-Paced: Recorded modules with certification exams
