Energy Management & Digital Product Passport System

About This Platform

The ENMS (Energy Management System) Demo Platform is a comprehensive demonstration environment showcasing cutting-edge Industry 4.0 technologies for energy monitoring, sustainability tracking, and digital product passport implementation.

This platform integrates real-time data collection, advanced analytics, and visualization tools to provide insights into manufacturing processes, energy consumption patterns, and environmental impact metrics.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with intelligent, data-driven solutions that optimize energy efficiency, reduce carbon footprint, and enable transparent product lifecycle management in alignment with global sustainability goals and Industry 4.0 standards.

Key Features

Real-Time Monitoring

Live energy consumption tracking across multiple devices and production lines with sub-second granularity.

Digital Product Passports

Complete product lifecycle documentation including energy usage, materials, and environmental impact.

AI-Powered Analytics

Machine learning algorithms for predictive maintenance and energy optimization recommendations.

Sustainability Metrics

Comprehensive carbon footprint calculation and ESG reporting aligned with international standards.

Enterprise Security

Role-based access control, encrypted data transmission, and audit logging for compliance.

IoT Integration

Seamless connectivity with sensors, PLCs, and industrial equipment via MQTT, Modbus, and OPC UA.

Technology Stack

Core Technologies

  • Grafana: Advanced visualization and dashboarding
  • Node-RED: IoT workflow automation and data integration
  • TimescaleDB: Time-series database for high-performance data storage
  • Python & Flask: Backend API and machine learning services
  • PostgreSQL: Relational database for structured data
  • Mosquitto MQTT: Message broker for IoT device communication
  • Docker: Containerization for scalable deployment

Target Users

This platform serves various stakeholders including:

  • Energy Managers: Monitor and optimize energy consumption across facilities
  • Production Managers: Track manufacturing efficiency and equipment performance
  • Sustainability Officers: Generate ESG reports and track environmental KPIs
  • Quality Assurance: Access digital product passports and compliance documentation
  • IT Administrators: Manage system configuration and user access
  • Executive Leadership: Strategic insights through high-level dashboards

Demo Environment

This is a fully functional demonstration environment with simulated data representing typical manufacturing scenarios. The system includes:

  • Multiple 3D printer fleets with realistic energy consumption patterns
  • Historical data covering various operational conditions
  • Pre-configured dashboards for different user profiles (Staff, Technical, DPP)
  • Sample digital product passports with complete lifecycle information
  • Interactive analysis tools for data exploration

Note: All data in this demo environment is simulated for demonstration purposes and does not represent actual production systems.

Getting Started

Explore the platform using the navigation menu on the left and the dashboard buttons in the top navigation bar. Each section provides different perspectives on energy management and sustainability metrics:

  • Fleet Operations: Overview of all connected devices and their status
  • Performance Comparison: Side-by-side analysis of equipment efficiency
  • Interactive Analysis: Deep-dive into historical trends and patterns
  • Digital Product Passport (DPP): Product-specific sustainability documentation
  • Node-RED: Configure IoT workflows and data pipelines
